Background Information

Note! This is an interactive tool that takes a lemma and outputs suggestions for simpler words that are similar in meaning.

The simplicity (complexity) of the word is based.This tool is a simplified version of our full system.

Some words might not occur in the corpus.

Note that we do not claim that this system is perfect, and you should only use the words suggested here as inspiration.
